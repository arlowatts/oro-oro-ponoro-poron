---

title: Bard

---

Bards seek to amaze and amuse with magic tricks, jolly songs, and humourous gags. They are experts of diversion, and can
calm or incite a riot with only a few words. Bards practice all the performing arts and must study acting, music,
comedy, and poetry.

## Abilities

### Look at Me!

You can get anyone's attention at any time as long as they can see or hear you, and you can keep their attention for up
to 5 seconds.

### Inspire

Once per day, you can inspire an emotion or feeling of your choice in a crowd of people who are watching you perform.
The emotion or feeling remains in your audience for up to 15 minutes after you stop performing.
