title: Krakakanamarakan

layout: page

---

Krakakanamarakan is the language spoken in Krakakanamar. It naturally evolved from three dialects originally spoken by
the Krankar, Nanak, and Ankankanakamakarakan clans who founded Krakakanamar.

Krakakanamarakan uses only four consonants: *k*, *r*, *n*, and *m*. Its only vowel is *a*. Most syllables start with a
consonant, end with a vowel, and have nothing in between: *ka*, *ra*, *na*, and *ma*. The last vowel of every word is
dropped, as in *Krakakanamar*. The special *kra* and *a* syllables can start a word, and the combination *nk* consonant
is permitted everywhere else, as in *Ankankanakamakarakan*.
