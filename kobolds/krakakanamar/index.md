Krakakanamar, also known as Krakakanamarakanaramanak, is a large and bustling city on the coast of Oro-oro-ponoro-poron.
Unlike the [[Bekebel]], [[Lukurru]], and [[Dagana]] clans, the people of Krakakanamar do not labour in the service of
dragons. Through long years of toil, they have carved out a free democratic society in a sheltered cove unbothered by
the other clans. Now, only 50 years after first breaking ground, Krakakanamar stands firm against the status quo on
Oro-oro-ponoro-poron and is a significant economic force on the island.
