Krakakanamar is a large and bustling city on the coast of Oro-oro-ponoro-poron. Unlike the {% a Bekebel %}, {% a Lukurru
%}, and {% a Dagana %} clans, the people of Krakakanamar do not labour in the service of dragons. Through long years of
struggle, they have carved out a free democratic society in a sheltered cove unbothered by the other clans. Now, only 40
years after first sprouting from its sandy bay, Krakakanamar stands firm against the status quo and represents a free
future heretofore unanticipated even by the clans' greatest visionaries.

Krakakanamar was founded by the nomadic Krankar and Nanak clans, who were later joined by the Ankankanakamakarakan clan.
Divisions between these families still separate Krakakanamar's three main districts.

The language spoken in Krakakanamr is {% a Krakakanamarakan %}.
