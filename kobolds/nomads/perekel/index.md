title: Perekel

---

The Perekel clan is a small nomadic clan frequently allied with the {% a Bekebel %}. Perekel is one of the few clans
which worships no dragon, drake, or wyvern.

{% a Janjan the Uncowed %}, the sister of {% a Tantan the Inventor %}, is a prominent figure in the clan despite being
an outsider.
