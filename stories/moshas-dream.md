{% a Mosha the All-knowing %} is commonly known among the {% a Bekebel %} as the {% a Lukurru %} clan's omniscient
dictator, and a terrifying enemy. Even since the two clans signed a treaty ensuring 81 years of peace, Mosha has
remained a vicious monster in Bekebel folklore.

But now the future of both clans is in doubt. To survive, you must overcome your ancient prejudices and join forces with
your sworn enemies!
